Turan Tour

Authentic Experiences

Turan Tour was founded in 2025 by a young couple based in Western Mongolia. We aim to develop our region, create new jobs for locals, and increase their household earnings. Our company organizes: mountain-climbing expeditions, hiking trips, horse treks, meetings with eagle hunters, visits to nomadic families, cultural tours, fishing trips, bird-watching, mountain-biking, and the Golden Eagle Festival tours. We have successfully raised funds from domestic and foreign supporters for projects and initiatives to improve the living standards of our local communities. We also contribute a certain percentage of our annual income to local projects and events. In 2018, Altai Expeditions LLC was selected as the “Best Tour Operator of the Year” by the Tourism Association of Bayan-Ulgii province.
